Accept payment with Heavycoin

Heavycoin is keen to become one of the top globally accepted digital payment systems. To this end, the Heavycoin Foundation is offering bounties for commerce websites accepting Heavycoin as payment for goods and services. The bounties are payable to whoever is responsible for the commerce site accepting Heavycoin as payment. It may be split between the operator of the commerce website and the Heavycoin promoter.
